Privacy Policy

Effective 2026

Anonymous Javascript Parameters

We collect data about the browser using standard javascript requests, as specified by the W3C. This is the same method used by Google Analytics, Adobe Analytics, and other standard analytics platforms. The data is used solely to determine whether a visitor is a bot or a human, and to detect fraudulent activity.

No Personal Data

This data contains NO personal information or personally identifiable information (PII). We never request information from users. IP addresses are treated as personally identifiable information for EU citizens and are not retained.

No Tracking Cookies

We do not set or use cookies in the determination of bots versus humans. Specifically:

  • No cookies are used for bot or human detection
  • No login credentials are required for the tracking functionality
  • No cross-session or cross-website user tracking
  • We are not an advertising technology company that does user targeting or retargeting

Note: The FouAnalytics dashboard login uses session cookies solely for authentication. This is entirely separate from the bot-detection technology.

No Sharing of Data

We are the sole owners of the information collected on this site. We will not sell or rent this collected information to anyone.

Security

We always use HTTPS to secure all communications to and from the browser and our servers. Data is encrypted during transmission and during storage.
